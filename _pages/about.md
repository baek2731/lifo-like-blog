## Who's Behind This?

I go by **LIFO** — the person running LIFO-LIKE. I've spent years following the tech and gaming industry closely — tracking product launches, policy shifts, and the occasional corporate meltdown that everyone saw coming except the company itself.

I've followed CES from afar and up close. I've watched E3 go from spectacle to ghost town. I've sat through enough "revolutionary" product demos to fill a graveyard of broken promises. That perspective is what LIFO-LIKE runs on.

## What LIFO-LIKE Is

This isn't a news aggregator. It isn't a press release rewriter.

LIFO-LIKE is where I break down the stories that actually matter in tech and gaming — the ones with real implications for your wallet, your privacy, or your next 60-hour gaming session. I scan what's trending globally, pick the signal from the noise, and write what I actually think.

No PR fluff. No "10 Reasons Why This Product Is Amazing" listicles written by someone who never touched the product.

## What I Cover

- **Tech industry moves** — AI policy, corporate decisions, hardware launches
- **Gaming** — industry news, publisher behavior, game announcements that actually matter
- **The intersection of both** — because in 2026, they're increasingly the same thing

## Editorial Standards

Every post on LIFO-LIKE reflects my own analysis and opinion. Editorial decisions are made independently. If a post contains an error, I correct it openly and promptly — corrections are noted directly in the article.

I aim to present all political and policy topics from a balanced, analytical perspective. No partisan framing. No ideological labels. Ideas are critiqued on their merits.
